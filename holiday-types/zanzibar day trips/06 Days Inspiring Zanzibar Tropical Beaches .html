  • North Zanzibar: 5 Nights at Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa

Did you know Africa has incredible tropical islands?

The tropical paradise of Zanzibar is located off the coast of Tanzania. Known as the spice island, Zanzibar is located on the off shore in the Indian Ocean and has some of the most spectacular beaches in the world.White sandy beaches, palm trees and the Indian Ocean at Matemwe in northern east island of Zanzibar. Here you will take you to Matemwe a village loved by locals and travelers for its seemingly never-ending wide and romantic colorless white sand beach. If you are looking to reconnect with nature and relax far away from it all, Matemwe is your spot to be. Here you will find pristine fishing communities, hosting few tourists and more fish markets. At the stalls, you can try local dishes.

Day 1: Arrival & Warm Welcoming In Zanzibar

On arrival at the Zanzibar Airport you will be met by our company representative and transferred to your hotel in North East – Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa overnight.


  • Overnight: Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa

  • Meals Plan: All Inclusive Package

    • Day 2: Zanzibar - Full day at Leisure

    Get relaxed and feel the breath taking atmosphere of the Indian Ocean at the beautiful white sandy beaches and turquoise water that gently rolls onto the beach. You can spend the entire day if you decide to get active after all, you can take a stroll on the romantically secluded beach – a wonderful diversion from long hours of relaxing at the hotel.


  • Overnight: Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa

  • Meals Plan: All Inclusive Package

    • Day 3: Half Day – Snorkeling with Bottlenose Dolphin; Mnemba Atoll

    One of the best places for snorkeling is Mnemba Atoll, Located North Eastern coast of the Island. A romantic hideaway, Mnemba Island is an exclusive paradise, Discover sheer, unpretentious paradise on this exclusive island, where the simple pleasures become your most treasured of memories for many years to come. Relax and unwind on your own stretch of beachfront, The most famous marine conservation area in Zanzibar. This atoll features an easy dive down to about 20 meters and quite a bit coral, you can also spot dolphins, whales, and sea turtles due to the deeper water in the seaSnorkeling in Mnemba Atoll offers you a stunning underwater with schools of tropical reef fish, turtles, dolphins, nudibranchs and beautiful hard and soft coral formations. Between the snorkeling session you can stay on the boats and taste fresh seasonal fruits, spice tea and snacks.


  • Overnight: Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa

  • Meals Plan: All Inclusive Package

    • Day 4: Private; Unforgettable Sea Adventure (Safari Blue)

    Picked up from the hotel, on a post breakfast drive will take you to Fumba Village, where you will hop on a traditional modernized dhow boat sailing to the breathtaking Minai Bay home to a cluster of beautiful, uninhabited islands. Once you reach the destination, the dhow boat will be anchored in an idyllic spot, allowing you to swim around, snorkeling along the rich coral reefs, spot the frolicking dolphins or explore the pristine islets. Your day will have an interrupted mouthwatering buffet lunch on Kwale Island, where you can tuck into succulent calamari, freshly grilled fish, huge lobster and juicy tropical fruits under the shade of massive tamarind trees. After a fantastic day out in the sun, you will return to Fumba Village on a lanteen sail back about the turbulent history of the Island.


  • Overnight: Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa

  • Meals Plan: All Inclusive Package

    • Day 5: Private; Prison Island and Visit Spice Plantation Farms

    After breakfast you will go to Stone Town where you will take a private boat ride to Prison Island; the Island is now home to the infamous prison ruins and tortoise sanctuary, famous for its giant tortoises. Observe the so intriguing creatures leisurely shuffling through the trees, whilst adventurous. After the Island trip you will take a short drive to Spice Farm for a Private Swahili Lunch. After lunch you will have time to go around the Spice plantation center for a short tour where you will be roaming through the fragrant spice plantations of Zanzibar and breathe in the scent of an infused past. Led by a friendly and professional guide, get an exclusive insight into the farms of this 3-hour tour. Nowadays the plantations are à tribute to the island’s colonial past. This is an excellent chance to learn about the turbulent history of the Island, later driving slowly back to Emerald Zanzibar Resort & Spa overnight.
