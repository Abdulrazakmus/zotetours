Zanzibar's Turtle Haven: Swim, Feed, and Sail with Sea Turtles on a Coastal Adventure


In the north of Zanzibar, you can find one of the natural lagoons full of sea turtles. At Baraka Aquarium they are buying the turtles, caught by fishermen and they are releasing them back to the fresh ocean water in the lagoon. Use the unique opportunity to swim with sea turtles. During the visit you can also feed turtles with sea weed or just spend your time in the water. Also, you can take a snorkeling mask to dive and watch them in their natural environment.


Enjoy your lunch then spend time at Kendwa Beach before hopping on a dhow wooden boat for your private sunset cruise along the coastal line approaching the sun setting. You can also spend more time snorkeling at your own leisure.
